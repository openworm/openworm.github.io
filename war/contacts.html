Join the discussion!

The easiest way to follow our day-to-day activities is to join our high volume mailing list that we use as a way to keep our communications in the open. We also have a lower volume mailing list we use for announcements and general discussion and inquiries.

For ongoing information about OpenWorm, please follow our blog on tumblr. You can also follow us and interact on twitter and Google+ for more frequent updates.

We'd love to hear from you! You should feel free to contact us with feedback, questions, partnership proposals, or other inquiries at infoatopenworm.org.
