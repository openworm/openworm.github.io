Who are you?

You'll find some of the most talented and friendliest folks in here, which means it's a great place to learn and a great place to give back, no matter your level of expertise.

Coder

We need your skills! No matter what programming language you are used to we are going to find a place for you! We are building different software components which require different expertise.
Eclipse is our official IDE for which we have customised distributions with all the plugins needed to get started.

Geppetto, our simulation platform, is written in JAVA and leverages technologies like OSGi, Spring Framework, OpenCL and Maven.
Geppetto's frontend is written using THREE.js and WebGL.
Back-end front-end communication happens transfering JSON messages throuhg WebSocket.
The engine runs on on Eclipse Virgo WebServer deployed on an Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud Linux instance.
Does any of these sound familiar and you would like to help? Contacts us!

Our most popular scripting language is Python which we use for many purposes, from hacking and managing documents quickly to interface with neuro-informatics resources. Even our optimisation engine is written in Python.
If you have experience with it you could surely help the project in a number of ways, so please contacts us!

Scientist

Dear fellow scientist, do you work with C. elegans in your lab? Do you work with optogenetics? Do you write computational models for neurons? Are you an expert in connectomics? Do you work with fluid dynamics simulations? Do you work with computational models for chemical diffusion? Do you have experience with numerical methods for solving cable equations? Do you think that you are working on something that we could be interested in?

If the answer to any of the above questions is yes and you would like to help us please contacts us!

Writer

So you are a writer! Whether you are a novelist, a blogger or a technical writer we could surely use a hand.
Communication is fundamental for us and we try our best to clearly convey what is our goal at OpenWorm and make people aware of the importance of what we are trying to do. Get in touch with us sending some samples of your writing and describe in what way you would like to contribute!

Also if you are writing a blog post or an article about the project feel free to ask us for any material like our logo or some sexy worm graphics, we would be more than happy to help you!

Artist

We love beautiful things. We always try to make an extra effort for things not only to have substance but also to look as cool as they are. Are you a graphic designer? Do you draw or do you make awesome videos? We need you. We love to inspire people and we believe art is the best way to do it.

If you would like to help out please send us some works from your portfolio and tell us how would you like to use your talent. If you can't think of anything still get in touch with us, we'll figure something out together!

WebMaster

Do you have experience as a WebMaster? Did you spot at first glance we built our website with Bootstrap? Do you speak javascript?

Mantaining our web site is an important job and we could surely use a hand. If you would like to help us contacts us linking some of the websites you worked on. Also feel free to give us any feedback on usability of this website, we like to constantly raise the bar!

Philantropist

Do you share our vision? Do you believe that to accelerate drug discovery we need better predictive model for biology? Are you an advocate of open science?

OpenWorm has not received any funding yet and everything that was done so far happened thanks to the passion and volunteer work of our people.
Imagine how fast we could accelerate our development with a monetary contribution.

If you are interested in helping to fund OpenWorm or partner with us please contacts us, we need your help.

Curious citizen

One thing we need more than anything is for people to know about us. Getting visibility over the project will help us attract attention, raise awarness and ultimately reach our goal faster.

If you would like to help us you can spread the word with your family and friends and explain them how a biological accurate simulation of a tiny worm could help a lot to accelerate cure of diseases. You can also tell your geek friends how it would be totally cool to have a virtual worm living inside your computer.

Also if there is anything you think you could help us with which doesn't fall in any of the above categories please contacts us, we would love to hear from you!
