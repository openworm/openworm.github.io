For coders




We packaged Eclipse distributions for the different operating systems, ready with all the plugins necessary to start working on Geppetto, our simulation engine.

Eclipse Distributions

We packaged Geppetto's prototype, including a basic frontend plotting demo data from a single compartment parallel solver for Hodgkin-Huxley neurons. Here's instructions.

Geppetto prototype

For scientists

We have captured the full 3D morphology of the C. elegans connectome in NeuroML format, and we are currently working to integrate electro-physiology.

NeuroML Connectome

We have optimized electro-phisyiology for a specific C. elegans muscle cell (we plan on doing this on the rest fo the cells too) in NeuroML format.

NeuroML Muscle cell

We are maintaining a spreadsheet that lists all C. elegans neurons along with neurotransmitters and neuropeptides. Anything to add? Give us a shout!
