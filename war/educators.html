Educational content


In their journey to simulating the first digital organism in a computer, OpenWorm volunteers have produced content and tools that can be useful for educators in teaching C. elegans biology.
Our series of educational videos about C. elegans explain why studying this model organism is so important and show how much wonder can hide inside a 1mm longer worm.
